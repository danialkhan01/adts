Martin Tran - Old Man Representative

My work explores the relationship between the challenges of ageing and midlife mindfulness at the intersection of achievement. With both personal experience and influences as diverse as a father and a grandfather, new synergies are synthesised from both personal and external experiences. Ever since I was a teenager I have been fascinated by the essential unreality of the human condition. What starts out as triumph soon becomes finessed into a spaghetti of daily lifestyle challenges, the stubbornness of ageing, leaving only a sense of failing and the possibility of a new synthesis.
